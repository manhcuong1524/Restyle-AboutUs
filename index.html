ABOUT

Founded in 2021, KnightOwl has been delivering on the promise to provide its valuable customer inexpensive aesthetic accessories for their gaming battlestations. Our first product line focuses on the focal point of any PC build - the GPU. By offering intuitive personalization technologies, KnightOwl enables gamers to explore and render their uniqueness and creativity onto its custom addressable RGB backplate. All of our products are designed and shipped from Anaheim, California.
